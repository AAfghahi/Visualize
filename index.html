Instructions

A couple of commands to customize your experience:
  • If you have a mp3 handy, upload it onto the player! Otherwise, feel free to use the demo song.
  • You can toggle the changings colors by pressing 'a'.
  • When color is toggled off, you can manually change colors by pressing 'c'.
  • You can zoom in and out using your mouse wheel, and you can rotate the entire screen by clicking and dragging!
Enjoy!
